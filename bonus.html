GoldenGriffin

Griffin is gigantic in size and brown in colour that has the head, forelegs and wings of an eagle and the hindquarters, tail and ears of a lion.It represents values such as strength, vigilance, and intelligence, all of which befit a college and qualities that one would look for in students and athletes alike.Golden Griffins are composed of 16 teams representing Austin College in Vacaville in intercollegiate athletics.These teams include men's and women's basketball.The men's basketball team has made four appearances in the NCAA Division I Men's Basketball Tournament, the last appearance coming in 1996.
